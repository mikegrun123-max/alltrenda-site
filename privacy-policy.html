Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

Alltrenda respects your privacy. This website and application are used to demonstrate a content automation system that generates and publishes short-form videos to social media platforms such as TikTok.

Information We Collect

When users authenticate through TikTok Login, the application may access basic profile information permitted by TikTok APIs, such as:

  • TikTok user ID
  • Basic account information
  • Video upload permissions

This information is used solely to enable the application to publish video content to the connected TikTok account.

How We Use Information

The information accessed is used only for:

  • Authenticating the TikTok account
  • Uploading video content through the TikTok Content Posting API
  • Managing content created by the developer

The application does not collect sensitive personal data and does not store personal user information outside the authorization process.

Third-Party Services

This application may interact with third-party services such as cloud storage providers and artificial intelligence tools used to generate video content.
